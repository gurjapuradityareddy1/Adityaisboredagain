The World of Black Ants
Feb 2026 · Biology

Black ants survive through division of labor and chemical intelligence. Worker ants — all female — build, defend, forage, and maintain the colony. Male ants exist only for reproduction and die shortly after mating.

Ants follow pheromone trails — invisible chemical paths laid on the ground. The stronger the trail, the more ants follow. Shorter routes get reinforced. Longer ones fade. No leader. Just decentralized optimization.
